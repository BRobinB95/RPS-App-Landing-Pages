How you play

Your rules.
Your pace.

Casual
Play to play.
Timer: 10 seconds
Format: Best of 5, 7, or 9 — you choose
Stakes: Coins only
Progress: Earns your Casual title

No pressure. Just matches. Good for warming up, testing new habits, or just not being in the mood for ranked.
Ranked
Play to climb.
Timer: 7 seconds
Format: Always best of 9
Stakes: RP gain or loss
Unlocks: After 10 casual games

This is where it counts. Every match moves your rating. Seasonal rewards go to those who earned them.
